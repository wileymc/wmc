#### Coffee
- Crema, Queen City, Milk and Honey, and Blue Sparrow Coffee are all great
- Close: Green Bus Cafe, Whittier Cafe

#### Restaurants
- **French**: Potager, Brasserie Brixton
- **Italian**: Jovanina's Broken Italian, Lou's Italian, White Pie, Shells and Sauce
- **Ramen:** Brother, Osaka, Mecha
- **Sushi**: Temaki Den, Sushi Den
- **Thai:** Alloy Modern Thai, US Thai Cafe
- **Chinese**: Zo Ma Ma
- **Vietnamese:** Sap Sua
- **Mediterranean**: Safta, Sonny's 
- **Seafood:** Fish and Beer, 
- **Mexican**: Mister Oso, La Loma, Luchador
- **Bagels:** Rosenburg's
- **Burgers:** Dalton's (smash), Grandpa's Burger Haven (greaser), Park Burger (classic)

#### Bars
- **Dives:** 715 club, The Dive Inn, Walter’s
- **Cocktails**: Yacht Club, Retrograde (cool speakeasy spot in an icecream shop), Welton Room, Marigold, Finn's Manor, The Thin Man
- **Breweries**: Spangalang, Woods Boss, Our Mutual Friend
- **Rooftop**: The Catbird Hotel, Marigold
- **Dancing**: Beacon, Resident, Milk

#### Live Music
- **Headliners:** Red Rocks, The Mission Ballroom
- **Live Jazz** on Mondays, Tuesdays, Wednesdays at Meadowlark
- **Brunch and a Show** weekends at Ophelia's Electric Soapbox
- Sunday nights at Yacht Club, always a great band
- Jazz in the park, most Sundays in City Park during the summer, 6-8pm
- Larimer Lounge usually has cool indie/electronic shows, nice low key bar and patio attached

#### Groceries
- **Local:** Sun Market (1 block walk)
- **Organic:** Natural Grocers, Whole Foods
- **Standard:** Safeway
